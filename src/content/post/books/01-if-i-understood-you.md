#### Top-down and bottom-up communication

Top-down communication is the BLUF (bottom line up front) style of communicating.
A top-down approach is more effective in consulting and business settings because
it focuses directly on the key results and findings that a C-level executive
would be interested in.

Bottom-up communication is when you explain more of the details about the process
that leads to a result rather than the result itself.
